IFBB PRO CARD 2018

To start, I made sure to have a well-rounded training plan that included both weightlifting and cardio exercises. I focused on progressive overload and proper form to build strength and avoid injury. I also followed a strict diet, paying close attention to macronutrient and calorie intake to ensure I had enough fuel for my workouts and to reach my desired physique.

  • In addition to my training and diet, I also made a point to consistently stay hydrated and get enough sleep. These aspects are often overlooked, but they play a crucial role in recovery and overall performance.
  • As the competition approached, I made sure to pace myself and not overtrain. I also visualized my routine and practiced posing to ensure that I was confident and ready for the stage.
  • On competition day, I entered the stage with confidence and put all my hard work on display. The judges were impressed with my muscle definition, symmetry, and stage presence.

This experience taught me that success takes more than just physical preparation. It also requires mental preparation and the discipline to stick to a plan. I hope that my story inspires others to chase their dreams and never give up on their goals.
